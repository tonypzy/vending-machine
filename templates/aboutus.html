About Vend-nier

This project is designed to help students and staff at The Ohio State University find the nearest and most convenient vending machines.

Our goal is to provide real-time data on machine locations, services (drinks, snacks), and accepted payment methods (including BuckID).

Technology

This application is built with Python (Flask) on the backend, Elasticsearch for data storage and geospatial search, and modern HTML/CSS/JavaScript with Leaflet.js on the frontend.
