About Vend-nier

This project is designed to help students, staff, and visitors at The Ohio State University locate the nearest and most convenient vending machines across campus.

Our goal is to provide real-time data on machine locations, specific services (drinks vs. snacks), and accepted payment methods (including BuckID) to save you time between classes.

Technology Stack

Vend-nier leverages a modern geospatial tech stack to deliver fast, accurate search results.

Python (Flask) Elasticsearch Render Cloud Google Gemini AI OpenAI ChatGPT OpenRouteService Interactive Mapping
